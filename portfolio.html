Beyond the Pandemic: A Deep Dive into Chloe Ting's YouTube Evolution

This project analyzes Chloe Ting's YouTube channel from 2020 to 2022, with a focus on understanding viewer engagement and the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic. By examining key metrics and trends, it aims to provide insights to enhance content strategies and adaptability in a dynamic digital landscape.
