My Foundation

Bridging Disciplines

My journey from Computer Science showed me the necessity of logical, modular architecture. It ensures designs are not just aesthetically pleasing but are efficiently deployable and maintainable at scale.

My background in Statistics taught me that every design decision must be validated by measurable user behavior. This analytical rigor transforms subjective preference into objective, data-driven optimization.

UX design is the practice where these disciplines converge, allowing me to build products that are technically sound, measurably effective, and a pleasure to use.
