Redesigning a shopping experience for Depop users.

Timeline
March 2024
PLATFORM
iOS App
MY ROLE
Product Designer

Introduction

Depop, a popular fashion resale platform, aims to connect buyers and sellers of unique and trendy clothing. However, the current Depop homepage might not be maximizing user engagement and item discovery. This case study explores the redesign process, focusing on improving user experience and driving sales.

‍

Problem

User research revealed several pain points:

‍

PROBLEM #1: Content Overload

The homepage felt cluttered, hindering item discovery.

‍

PROBLEM #2: Limited Personalization

Users struggled to find relevant items based on their preferences.

‍

PROBLEM #3: Lack of Trust Signals

Uncertainty about seller reliability and item condition discouraged purchases.

‍

Goal

Enhance user engagement with the homepage.


Personalize content to improve item discovery.


Increase user trust through transparency.
