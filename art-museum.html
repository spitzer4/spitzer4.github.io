Designing an enhanced experience for museum patrons.

Timeline
February 2024 - March 2024
PLATFORM
iOS App
MY ROLE
Product Designer

Introduction

With museums experiencing declining visitor numbers in recent years, this UX case study focuses on the development of a museum app aimed at enhancing the visitor experience. By leveraging technology, the app aims to bridge the gap between traditional museum visits and modern expectations, revitalizing interest in cultural institutions and fostering deeper engagement with art and heritage.

‍

Problem

Here are the key problems with the user experience of museum visitors:

‍

PROBLEM #1: Lack of Interactivity

Many museum visitors experience a lack of engagement due to static exhibits and limited interactive elements, resulting in diminished interest and shortened visit durations.

‍

PROBLEM #2: Frustration with Navigation

Visitors often feel frustrated when navigating through museums, as they struggle with getting lost or disoriented within the expansive and labyrinthine layout of galleries and exhibits.

‍

PROBLEM #3: Overwhelm with Direction

Visitors frequently feel overwhelmed by the sheer volume of exhibits and information, unsure of where to start or what to focus on during their visit, leading to decision paralysis and a disjointed museum experience.

‍

Defining the Design Problem

Create a museum app that addresses the key challenges faced by visitors, including boredom due to lack of interactivity, frustration with navigation, and overwhelm with direction, ultimately enhancing the overall visitor experience and engagement within museum spaces.

‍

Goal

Enhance Engagement

Visitors aim to engage more deeply with museum exhibits and collections, seeking interactive and immersive experiences that capture their interest and curiosity.


Seamless Navigation

Users aspire to navigate museum spaces effortlessly, without getting lost or disoriented, to maximize their time and enjoyment during their visit.


Personalized Experience

Visitors desire a tailored and personalized museum experience, including curated itineraries and recommendations that align with their interests and preferences.


Learning and Discovery

Users strive to expand their knowledge and understanding of art and culture through meaningful learning opportunities and discovery-driven interactions within museum exhibits.


Enjoyment and Satisfaction

Visitors seek to enjoy a fulfilling and satisfying museum visit, characterized by exploration, discovery, and a sense of accomplishment in engaging with museum content.


Users

Elena Rodriguez

Elena, an art professor, navigates the challenges of balancing work and family commitments. Frustrated by time constraints, she aims to efficiently plan enriching family museum visits that cater to her children's diverse interests, fostering a love for art.

‍

Aisha Khan

Aisha, a first-year computer science student and part-time tech-support worker, strives to balance her tech-savvy interests with a passion for art. Frustrated with time constraints that limit physical museum time, she seeks a seamless digital experience that allows her to explore art.


Process

The problem at hand is to address the challenges faced by museum visitors by designing a user-friendly museum app that enhances the overall visitor experience.
