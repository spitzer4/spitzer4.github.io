UnBoxed

Simplified Sustainable Shopping

Solo Project
My Role: Product Designer
Timeline: March 2025 - April 2025

A zero waste shopping app aimed at simplifying the eco-friendly shopping experience for environmentally conscious users. The app aims to bridge the gap between intent and action for those looking to reduce their environmental footprint.

Problem

Sustainability is becoming increasingly important to consumers, yet navigating the eco-friendly shopping space remains confusing and overwhelming. Users often face several challenges:

Solution

UnBoxed addresses these challenges by creating a centralized, user-friendly platform where users can easily find trustworthy, sustainable products based on their needs. Key features include:

  • Trustworthy Listings: Products are curated to ensure they meet sustainable standards, reducing the risk of greenwashing.
  • Beginner Guidance: A dedicated section for newcomers with simple, budget-friendly eco-friendly alternatives.
  • Convenience & Time-Saving: With personalized recommendations, users can shop for sustainable products that suit their values and lifestyles with minimal effort.
  • Aesthetic & Functional Design: Combining visually appealing design with intuitive functionality to drive adoption among eco-conscious consumers.

Impact

By focusing on user needs and addressing key pain points, UnBoxed provides measurable value:

  • Improved User Experience: Users report finding eco-friendly products more easily, with reduced mental load and frustration.
  • Increased Adoption of Sustainable Habits: With clear guidance, both beginners and experienced eco-conscious shoppers are more confident in making sustainable choices.
  • Empowerment Through Trust: The app's transparency and curated product list have fostered trust, increasing users' likelihood of recommending the platform to others.
