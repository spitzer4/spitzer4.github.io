UnBoxed

Simplified Sustainable Shopping

Solo Project
My Role: Product Designer
Timeline: March 2025 - April 2025

A zero waste shopping app aimed at simplifying the eco-friendly shopping experience for environmentally conscious users. The app aims to bridge the gap between intent and action for those looking to reduce their environmental footprint.

PROBLEM

Sustainable Living, Complicated Shopping.

Imagine wanting to make a difference, to shop sustainably, but being met with a maze of confusing labels and overwhelming choices. It's frustrating, isn't it? That's the feeling many eco-conscious consumers experience when trying to navigate the world of sustainable shopping.

SOLUTION

Ethical Shopping, Clearly Defined.

Our solution was UnBoxed, a mobile application designed to simplify sustainable shopping through personalization and community. As the lead Product Designer, I focused on creating an intuitive user experience that prioritized transparency and trust. The app features:

Transparent Product Information

  • Clear details on certifications, materials, and ethical sourcing.

Community Forum

  • A space for users to share reviews, tips, and connect with like-minded individuals.

Streamlined Checkout

  • An efficient process that minimizes friction.

Impact

By focusing on user needs and addressing key pain points, UnBoxed provides measurable value:

  • Improved User Experience: Users report finding eco-friendly products more easily, with reduced mental load and frustration.
  • Increased Adoption of Sustainable Habits: With clear guidance, both beginners and experienced eco-conscious shoppers are more confident in making sustainable choices.
  • Empowerment Through Trust: The app's transparency and curated product list have fostered trust, increasing users' likelihood of recommending the platform to others.
