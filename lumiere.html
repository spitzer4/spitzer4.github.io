Case Study: Designing a Luxury Fragrance Website for Lumiere

At a Glance

Project: Luxury fragrance brand website for Lumiere

Role: UX/UI Designer & Web Developer

Tools Used: Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Deliverables: High-fidelity prototype, responsive website, branding guide

Problem

Luxury fragrance brands often struggle to translate their sophistication and exclusivity into an engaging digital experience. Many high-end fragrance websites lack a seamless UX, fail to effectively communicate brand storytelling, or do not optimize for user engagement and conversion.

Solution

We designed a visually rich, intuitive, and conversion-optimized website that:

  • Elevates brand perception through refined typography, color, and layout
  • Enhances storytelling with engaging product descriptions and imagery
  • Encourages conversions through clear CTAs and an engaging newsletter signup

Process

Persona

Name: Isabelle Laurent

Age: 32

Occupation: Art Director at a luxury fashion brand

Pain Points: Overwhelming e-commerce layouts, impersonal shopping experiences

Needs: A seamless, elegant, and informative way to explore and purchase premium fragrances

User Journey Map

  • Awareness: "I need a new signature scent." - Browses luxury fragrance brands
  • Consideration: "Lumière looks elegant and high-end." - Clicks on hero CTA
  • Decision: "This scent sounds perfect for me." - Adds to cart
  • Retention: "I want to know about new launches." - Signs up for the newsletter

Paper Wireframes

Early sketches focused on:

  • Hero section layout with bold typography
  • Grid-based product displays
  • An immersive "About" section with storytelling elements

From Paper to Digital

Wireframes were digitized in Figma, refining layout and spacing while incorporating the luxury color palette.

User Testing & Design Changes

v1.0 → v2.0

Feedback: Text felt dense, social links were hard to find, and CTAs needed more engagement.

Changes:

  • Reformatted the "About" section for better readability
  • Increased size of social links for visibility
  • Updated newsletter signup title:
    Before: “Newsletter Signup” → After: *“Get Exclusive Access to New Scents & Special Offers”*
  • Enhanced CTA:
    Before: “Discover Scents” → After: *“Find Your Scent”*

v2.0 → v3.0

Feedback: Some text still lacked emphasis, product descriptions were missing, and CTA could be more enticing.

Changes:

  • Bolded key words in “About” section for emphasis
  • Added descriptions to scents for better storytelling
  • CTA update:
    Before: “Find Your Scent” → After: *“Discover Your Signature Scent”*

Low-Fidelity Prototypes

Wireframes focused on:

  • Content hierarchy (hero image, product display, CTA placement)
  • Navigation flow (intuitive menu, scent descriptions)
  • Mobile responsiveness

Design Changes & Final Touches

  • Refined luxury aesthetic using subtle hover animations
  • Implemented smooth scrolling for a seamless experience
  • Optimized SEO & performance for better visibility

Final Result

A high-end, immersive e-commerce experience that reflects Lumiere's luxurious brand identity, enhances user engagement, and drives conversions.
