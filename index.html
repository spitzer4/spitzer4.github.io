About Me

Hi! I’m Kaleigh, a UX/UI designer with a background in Computer Science and Statistics.

After working as a data analyst, I discovered my true passion lies in designing intuitive digital experiences that blend creativity with analytical thinking. My approach is deeply research-driven: I love digging into user needs, prototyping thoughtful solutions, and testing designs to make sure they truly work.

Coming from a technical background means I’m comfortable bridging the gap between design and development, turning ideas into functional, user-centered products backed by real data insights.

Outside of work, you’ll usually find me painting, practicing yoga, or exploring new ways to bring creativity and calm into my everyday life.
