About Me

I'm a passionate UX/UI designer who believes great design should make complex problems feel simple and enjoyable to solve. My approach combines user-centered design principles with creative problem-solving to create digital experiences that truly resonate with users.

With a background in both design and development, I bring a unique perspective to every project, ensuring that beautiful designs are not only visually appealing but also technically feasible and user-friendly.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new design trends, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying the vibrant culture of Chicago.
