Website Design

Crafting Modern Comfort Online

Solo Project

My Role

Product Designer

Timeline

January 2025 - February 2025

Tools

Figma

Nest is a fictional modern and cozy furniture brand, and this project aimed to design an e-commerce website that embodies its philosophy of “modern living, warmly crafted.” My goal was to create an intuitive, visually appealing online shopping experience that highlights the brand's unique aesthetic while ensuring a seamless user journey.

As the UX/UI designer for this project, I led the entire design process—from user research and wireframing to visual design and prototyping.

Goals

  • Design a clean and modern e-commerce website that embodies the brand's "modern living, warmly crafted" philosophy.
  • Create an intuitive and seamless shopping experience for users.
  • Develop a visually compelling product grid and product detail pages.
  • Establish a consistent brand identity through typography, color palette, and imagery.
  • Design a responsive layout that adapts to various screen sizes.

01 DISCOVER

What makes a great online shopping experience?

Competitive Analysis

Key Takeaways:

  • Users expect a clean, uncluttered layout with easy navigation.
  • High-quality product imagery is crucial for engagement.
  • A well-designed filtering system enhances the shopping experience.
  • Storytelling and branding play a key role in customer connection.

02 EMPATHIZE

Understanding the target audience.

User Persona

Meet Emily, a Typical Nest Customer

Age: 32

Needs: Stylish, well-crafted furniture that fits her aesthetic.

Pain Points:

  • Overwhelming product choices in traditional e-commerce sites.
  • Difficulty visualizing furniture in her space.
  • Unclear product descriptions and lack of detailed images.

Emily's frustrations helped inform the design direction, ensuring a smooth and delightful shopping experience.

03 IDEATE

Brainstorming and conceptualizing the design.

With Emily's needs in mind, I brainstormed features that would make Nest's website both functional and visually appealing.

Key Design Concepts:

  • Minimalist Layout: A calm and modern interface that lets products shine.
  • Emphasis on Imagery: Large, high-quality product photos to showcase details.
  • Seamless Navigation: Clear categories and an intuitive filtering system.
  • Brand Storytelling: A homepage that evokes warmth and craftsmanship.

I then sketched low-fidelity wireframes to establish the website's structure, focusing on usability and information hierarchy.

04 PROTOTYPE

Bringing the design to life.

Version 1.0

The initial design focused on:

  • A minimalist layout with ample white space.
  • A visually engaging product grid.
  • Clear navigation and filtering options.
  • A simple newsletter signup form.

However, after self-critique and feedback, I saw room for improvement.

Version 2.0

  • Enhanced Visual Hierarchy: Increased font sizes for better readability.
  • Stronger Brand Messaging: A more descriptive tagline emphasizing Nest's unique selling proposition.
  • Navigation Improvements: Added a Home link for better usability.

Alongside these refinements, I developed high-fidelity mockups to bring the design to life.

Style Guide

  • Color Palette: Neutral with warm undertones for a cozy, modern feel.
  • Typography: A balance of clean sans-serif for readability and elegant serif for warmth.
  • Imagery: High-quality product photography to highlight craftsmanship.

05 REFLECT

Lessons learned and key takeaways.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Balancing Modern & Cozy Aesthetic
  • Solution: A neutral palette with warm accents + elegant typography.
  • Ensuring Intuitive Navigation
  • Solution: A simple filtering system and clear product categories.

Final Thoughts

This project reinforced the importance of user-centric design in e-commerce. By focusing on the needs of the target audience, I created an online shopping experience that is both visually appealing and highly functional.

Would I make further improvements? Absolutely. Given more time, I'd explore:

  • User testing to refine the shopping flow.
  • Augmented reality previews for better product visualization.

Nest's e-commerce design demonstrates my ability to blend UX principles with strong branding to create a seamless and engaging user experience.
