Elixr UX/UI Design Case Study

Project Overview

Elixr is a brand that offers non-alcoholic beverages made from natural botanicals, designed to provide energy without the crash while being hydrating and refreshing. The goal of this project was to design a visually appealing and user-friendly website that highlights the product's unique selling points and encourages users to explore and purchase.

1. Research & Problem Definition

The Challenge

  • Many non-alcoholic beverage brands struggle with differentiating themselves in a competitive market.
  • Users need clear and engaging messaging that explains the benefits of Elixr at a glance.
  • The website must have a seamless shopping experience with an intuitive user flow.

User Research & Insights

  • ✅ Users want quick access to product details and benefits.
  • ✅ Aesthetic appeal is important, as buyers are drawn to visually vibrant and trendy branding.
  • ✅ Navigation should be simple and direct, avoiding unnecessary clutter.
  • ✅ Mobile responsiveness is critical, as many shoppers browse on their phones.

2. Design Goals & UX Strategy

  • 🔹 Minimalist Navigation - Keeping it simple with Home, Products, Contact, and a prominent “Shop Now” button.
  • 🔹 Strong Visual Hierarchy - Hero section immediately grabs attention with a bold tagline.
  • 🔹 Clear Call-to-Actions (CTAs) - Buttons like “Find Your Flavor” and “Shop Now” guide users toward conversions.
  • 🔹 Emphasis on Product Benefits - Features like “Natural Botanicals” and “Energy Without the Crash” are highlighted for quick readability.
  • 🔹 Mobile-First Approach - Ensuring easy navigation and readability on all screen sizes.

3. UI Design Process

Typography & Colors

  • Font: Clean, modern sans-serif for a fresh, approachable feel.
  • Colors: Earthy, vibrant tones reflecting botanical ingredients (e.g., greens, oranges, and neutral tones).
  • Imagery: High-quality product visuals with a lifestyle-driven approach.

Wireframing & Prototyping

  1. 1️⃣ Low-fidelity wireframes mapped out the core structure.
  2. 2️⃣ High-fidelity mockups refined visual details, typography, and color balance.
  3. 3️⃣ Clickable prototype created in Figma for usability testing.

4. Usability Testing & Iterations

  • 🚀 Users loved the bold tagline in the hero section but wanted a slightly larger CTA button.
  • 📱 Mobile users requested easier access to the Shop Now button, so it was placed in the sticky navigation bar.
  • 🛒 Some users expected to see product images earlier, leading to a slight homepage layout tweak.

4. Final Design & Development Handoff

  • ✅ Aesthetic yet functional layout that enhances brand storytelling.
  • ✅ Optimized navigation for fast and easy browsing.
  • ✅ Performance-focused development with lightweight assets for quick loading speeds.

5. Results & Takeaways

🎯 Expected Impact: Increased conversion rates, improved user engagement, and stronger brand perception.

Key Learnings:

  • 📌 Users prioritize clarity and efficiency over excessive design elements.
  • 📌 A strategic CTA placement significantly improves engagement.
  • 📌 Balancing aesthetics with usability is essential for an effective UX/UI experience.

Next Steps

  • ✔️ Implement A/B testing on different CTA placements to optimize conversion rates.
  • ✔️ Expand the product page with interactive elements like reviews or flavor quizzes.
  • ✔️ Continue refining based on real user behavior data post-launch.
